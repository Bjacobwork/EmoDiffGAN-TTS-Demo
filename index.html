EmotionDiffGAN-TTS Samples

This model presents a novel approach to emotional text-to-speech synthesis, leveraging the strengths of DiffGAN-TTS while incorporating fine-grained emotional control. The base model is modified to accept emotion vectors as input, conditioning both the Variance predictor within the generator and the convolutional blocks of the discriminator. These emotion vectors are derived from a pre-trained model capable of predicting sentiment and arousal from speech. This allows the model to generate speech with a wide range of emotional expressions.

This project is available on github.
